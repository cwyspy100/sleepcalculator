About Sleep Cycles

🌙 Sleep Cycles

Human sleep consists of 90-minute cycles including light sleep, deep sleep, and REM stages.

⏰ Optimal Sleep Duration

Adults should sleep 7-9 hours per night, equivalent to 5-6 complete sleep cycles.

😴 Time to Fall Asleep

Most people need 10-20 minutes to fall asleep, recommend allowing 15 minutes.

🌅 Wake-up Timing

Waking at the end of a sleep cycle will make you feel more alert and energized.

Why Sleep Cycles Matter

Sleep cycles are crucial for quality rest. Each cycle lasts approximately 90 minutes and includes different stages of sleep.
